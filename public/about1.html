We are a voluntary non-governmental organization that has recognized the genuine problems faced by the youth today, in terms of financial constraints, psychological incompatibility, peer pressure, societal burden, lack of infrastructure, absence of proper mentoring or guidance and information dissemination. Mauli-NGO intends to create leaders amongst youth on a national level offering a range of solution through our innovative products and services, mentoring programs and corporate tie ups. We always put student interests at the heart of our activities as we campaign for change. We aim to provide youth a platform wherein they shall be able to recognize their purpose of life and help them move closer to it, help them harness their potentials, channelize their resources, let them dream of higher pursuits and help them engage into a range of activities. We work at the grass root level and aim to empower the youth by encouraging them to face their fears and challenges, to think big and pursue their dreams.
